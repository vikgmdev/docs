Welcome to Range!

You're on your way to accessing our industry leading stablecoin, analysis and risk management tools!

This page will help you sign up to the Range platform and help you to get an API key.

## Getting an API Key

To use Range's platform you will need an API key. Don't worry it is simple and free to get one and takes about 30 seconds.

To get started head to [https://app.range.org](https://app.range.org).

Next click "Sign Up" in the bottom left of the screen.

<Image align="center" border={false} src="https://files.readme.io/9d292b033d5c0fa716c1956953c06a9c8c7aae746393c1577f05255b1ac73a2f-image.png" />

<br />

On the next screen if you have a Google account click "Sign in with Google" and authenticate with Google.

If you don't have a Google account you can sign up with an email address. With this method you'll receive a link to your email address to complete the signup process.

<Image align="center" border={false} width="400px" src="https://files.readme.io/eaaf7b6bcca676799fd9e292007209f0295a95d3d1892c8cf6a300b3981ff153-image.png" />

After this you are signed into the Range platform.

In the left hand navigation click "APIs"

<Image align="center" border={false} width="300px" src="https://files.readme.io/c9698bff6a438b2cb882296bd179448c54fe8a7b19cfbee2be41e74d61fa8add-image.png" />

On the APIs page you will see an API key has been generated for you and is ready to use on the Range Platform.

<Image align="center" border={false} width="600px" src="https://files.readme.io/717e968933ad56621530fff014ba37605235cc344e9e99e16ed2f902059197ed-image.png" />
