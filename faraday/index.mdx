Faraday makes using stablecoins safe, easy and compliant for everyone.

* Get the best price for stablecoin tranfers
* Manage risk including sanctioned addresses, address poisoning and common human error
* Maintain Travel Rule compliance
* Maintain historical record keeping

Faraday bundles swapping, risk management, compliance and reporting into the go to product for stablecoin users.

## Use Cases

Faraday enables developers, protocols, and institutions to build compliant, risk-aware cross-chain applications that deliver best execution across multiple aggregators while maintaining full regulatory assurance.

<details>
  <summary><big><strong>Stablecoin remittances</strong></big></summary>

  <p>DAOs, protocols, fintechs, and enterprises can use Faraday to execute cross-chain stablecoin transfers with the best available execution price. Every transaction is screened for sanctions, bad actors, and other compliance risks before execution, with full audit trails for transparency.</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary><big><strong>Treasury management</strong></big></summary>

  <p>On-chain treasuries can automate rebalancing, diversify stablecoin exposure, and manage liquidity across networks using Faraday. Each transaction is evaluated for counterparty and jurisdictional risk, ensuring compliant capital management and simplified historical reporting.</p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary><big><strong>Cross-chain payments</strong></big></summary>

  <p>Protocols and payment platforms can route large stablecoin payments efficiently across chains and aggregators, while maintaining Travel Rule compliance. Faraday optimizes for the best path and lowest cost, ensuring transfers are executed safely and verifiably.</p>
</details>

Faraday is non-custodial, with users in control of funds at all times.
